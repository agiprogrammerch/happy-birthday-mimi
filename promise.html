約束 · Yakusoku · A Promise

一 🌸

We Will See the Sakura

Somewhere in Japan, in the spring, we'll walk under cherry blossoms. Maybe Kyoto. Maybe Ueno Park in Tokyo. Maybe somewhere smaller and quieter that we stumble upon. But we'll be there. This is the first promise.

二 🍜

We Will Eat Ramen Together

Real ramen. At a small place. The kind where you sit at a counter and it's warm and the broth is exactly right. We'll probably argue about which bowl was better. This is the second promise.

三 🏯

And Then HK

Because your roots go two places — Japan and China, but you know a lot about Hong Kong — and a promise that only goes halfway isn't complete. We'll do both. Different trips, maybe. But both. This is the third promise.

四 🤝

I Will Keep This Promise

I don't say things I don't mean. So when I said we'd go together someday — I meant it. Life is long. Plans take time. But promises made honestly don't expire. This is the fourth promise, and the most important one.
